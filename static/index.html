Say Hi to Cloves!

A Simple chatbot messenger built with messenger

1. Say Hi!

To start, say hi to Clove!

2. Tell us your name

Introduce your self

3. When is your birthday?

Tell us the special date! (e.g. 2020-02-02)

4. Count the days!

Choose to count the days to your birthday

5. Endpoints

GET /messages to discover all the messages sent!

GET /messages/:id to discover the message with id

DELETE /messages/:id to delete the message with id
